Product Review Prompt (from Chapter 3) 

Act as a world-class copywriter specializing in affiliate product reviews.
Write a 1,200–1,500 word, SEO-optimized review for .
Audience: <TARGET>. Tone: <eg. honest, slightly skeptical>.

Structure:
1) Hook intro with core problem
2) What is <PRODUCT>?
3) Personal story & results (credibility)
4) Features → benefits (deep dive)
5) Pros & Cons (be candid)
6) Who is this PERFECT for?
7) Who should NOT buy this?
8) 3 Best Alternatives (quick compare)
9) Final Verdict & score

Add internal links where natural, 3 CTAs placed contextually,
and a skimmable summary box at the end.

Email Sequence Prompt (from Chapter 5) 

You are an expert email marketer. Create a 5-day PAS sequence for <PRODUCT>.
Goal: trust first, sales second. Avoid pushy tone.

Day 1 – Problem
Subject: “Struggling with <PROBLEM>?”
Body: Empathy + vivid pain scenario + tease solution.

Day 2 – Agitate
Subject: “The hidden cost of not fixing <PROBLEM>”
Body: Consequences (time, money, energy, risk).

Day 3 – Solve
Subject: “A practical way to fix <PROBLEM>”
Body: Introduce <PRODUCT> with 3 benefit bullets + soft CTA.

Day 4 – Social Proof
Subject: “How <CUSTOMER> solved <PROBLEM>”
Body: Brief case story, numbers, quote, CTA.

Day 5 – Urgency
Subject: “Last chance to grab <BONUS/DISCOUNT>”
Body: Recap value, handle objections, hard CTA, deadline.

TikTok Script Prompt (from Chapter 6) 

Generate a 30-second TikTok script promoting <PRODUCT>:
Format: “3 Reasons <PRODUCT> is a Game-Changer”

0–2s: Visual Hook (pattern interrupt) + on-screen text.
2–8s: Reason #1 (benefit + tiny proof).
8–16s: Reason #2 (problem → solution link).
16–24s: Reason #3 (unique angle or social proof).
24–30s: CTA – “Grab it via my bio before it’s gone.”

Include:
• Text overlays for each beat
• Short B-roll ideas or shot list
• Upbeat music cue (non-copyright library)
• Voiceover line for each scene
